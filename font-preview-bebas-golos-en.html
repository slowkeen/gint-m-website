Recommendation letter from Winline

Winline thanks the construction company Gint-M for delivering a corporate office project located at Yakor business center, 2 Presnenskaya Embankment. The total project area was 1,800 sq. m.

The Gint-M team acted as general contractor on the project, delivering the full scope of general construction, fit-out, and engineering works, as well as detailed design.

The team delivered the project at a high professional level despite a number of objective challenges: difficult distribution of certain materials and equipment from Europe under pandemic restrictions, as well as client-initiated changes that led to partial redesign of the office. Even so, the project was completed ahead of the agreed schedule and without compromises in quality.

Special mention should be made of the non-standard solutions in the project: complex ceiling structures and systems, covered skirting details, and an unconventional approach to ventilation and air conditioning. The contractor looked for the best possible options rather than taking the path of least resistance, and proposed truly effective solutions that made the Winline office technically well thought-out and genuinely comfortable.

The Gint-M team communicates effectively not only with the client, but also with building services, quickly coordinating all required works, as well as with the subcontractors involved in the project. As a result, all work progressed in strict accordance with the schedule and without organizational disruptions.

Based on the above, we describe the Gint-M team as a professional and reliable partner in construction and recommend it to everyone aiming to deliver a quality project within a predictable timeline and an agreed budget.

Sergey Ovcharov

Technical Director
